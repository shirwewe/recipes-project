A Buoyant Breeze Recipe

Ingredients

  • tomatoes 350g
  • potatoes 350g
  • onion 180g
  • green peas 180g
  • garlic cloves 20g
  • carrots 180g
  • thyme 2g
  • black pepper 3g
  • salt 3g
    •

Steps

  • Chop all ingredients
  • Add 50ml of olive oil to the pan, add in shredded onion and 10g of minced garlic; saut√© until fragrant;
  • Add carrots, potatoes, and peas, and fry evenly;
  • Pour in chopped tomatoes. If you prefer to use canned tomato paste, you can simply halve the tomatoes. If your tomatoes are sour, add a little sugar;
  • Add the remaining 10g minced garlic, black pepper, and salt
  • Transfer to a casserole pot and add thyme.
  • Simmer on low heat for 45 minutes
