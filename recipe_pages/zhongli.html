Slow-Cooked Bambooshoots Recipe

Ingredients

  • ham 227g/pack
  • pork belly 250g
  • bamboo shoot 150gM
  • Huadiao wine 20ml
  • Goji berry 5g
  • ginger 5g
  • shallot 1pc
    •

Steps

  • pork belly, cut into the size of mahjong pieces; Cut Jinhua ham into cubes; Peel and cut bamboo shoots;Fold beancurd into knots.
  • Put pork belly and Jinhua ham into a pot of cold water, and bring to a boil. Avoiding the foam, remove the meat; Put tofu knots and bamboo shoots into a fresh pot of boiling water
  • Put pork belly and ham in a clay casserole pot; One hour later, the entire house is filled with fragrance, open the lid and add the bamboo shoots; Continue to simmer for 15 minutes, finally, add the tofu knots and goji berries, simmer for 15 minutes to complete
